Chicken Noodle Soup

This homemade chicken noodle soup has a hint of southern spice that is sure to delight! Whip it up in under an hour with fresh vegetables, your favorite stock, and a bbq rotisserie chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 rotisserie chicken, preferably bbq flavor but any will do
  • 4 carrots, diced
  • 1 large sweet onion, sliced
  • 6 stalks of celery, diced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 head of garlic
  • Tony Chacheree's or other cajun seasoning to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
