Chicken Noodle Soup

This homemade chicken noodle soup has a hint of southern spice that is sure to delight! Whip it up in under an hour with fresh vegetables, your favorite stock, and a bbq rotisserie chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 rotisserie chicken, preferably bbq flavor but any will do
  • 2-3 quarts chicken stock
  • 4 carrots, diced
  • 1 large sweet onion, sliced
  • 6 stalks of celery, diced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 head of garlic
  • Tony Chacheree's or other cajun seasoning to taste
  • A few squirts of your favorite hot sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 table spoon butter
  • Shredded pepperjack cheese

Preparation

  1. Use your hands to tear the rotisserie chicken into smaller pieces. Use as much of it as possible. Set aside.
  2. Cut your veggies, keeping the onion and garlic separate.
  3. Heat butter in large soup pan until melted, then saute onions and garlic, adding a small amount of salt and pepper.
  4. Throw celery and carrots into the saute mixture for a minute or so.
  5. Pour stock into pan, add bay leaf, pepper, and Tony Chacheree's to taste. Bring to a simmer.
  6. Let simmer for about 10 minutes, then add chicken.
  7. Continue simmering to taste and texture preference.
  8. Add hot sauce to taste.
  9. After serving, add shredded pepperjack cheese as a topping.
